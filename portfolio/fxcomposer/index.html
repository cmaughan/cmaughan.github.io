NVIDIA FX Composer

FX Composer 2.5 is a powerful integrated development environment for shader authoring.

At NVIDIA I did a prototype shader IDE called FXComposer. It became a bigger project with many folks working on it. Eventually, when shader tools from other companies came on line, the project hit its final release.

We wrote many interesting shaders and effects, and with a big team behind it, there were lots of features for experimenting, including ways to measure your shader performance.

There were many interesting things I learned working on this project, such as:

  • C#/.NET development
  • IronPython/Python (embedded in the Application)
  • Building a more complex IDE
